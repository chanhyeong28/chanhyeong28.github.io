Financial Engineering & Algorithmic Trading

I am Chanhyong Ryu, a dedicated student at Seoul National University with a deep passion for algorithmic trading, particularly in delta-one strategies and options trading.

My research focuses on developing sophisticated quantitative models and trading algorithms that leverage advanced statistical methods, machine learning techniques, and real-time market data analysis. I specialize in creating systematic approaches to market inefficiencies, particularly in cryptocurrency derivatives and traditional financial instruments.

Through my projects, I explore the intersection of financial theory, computational methods, and market microstructure to develop robust trading strategies that can adapt to changing market conditions.
